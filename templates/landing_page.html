What's Alexandria?

Rentals

Rent textbooks from Alexandria Books and have them delivered to your door. Don't worry about shipping your books back, we'll pick them up at the end of the semester.

Textbook Buy Back

Have old textbooks sitting around? Done with classes and need some spending money? Alexandria will give you cash for you used book. We'll pick those up too.

Textbooks made simple.

Selection

Simply search for books by title, isbn or subject. Alexandria features a clean and simplified interface. No reviews. No recommendations. Can't find what you're looking for? Fill out the request form and we'll order it for you.

Payment

For rentals, we accept all major credit cards in addition to Paypal, Venmo, Square Cash and Bitcoin. Alexandria will buyback textbooks with your preferred payments platform.

Delivery/Pickup

Unless otherwise specified, all pick-ups and deliveries will be made on Saturdays. When completing your order choose the best 3 hr block that works for you. In a hurry? Contact us for special deliveries and expedited rates.

How it works.

Rent Sell
{# #######Rent####### #}

Shop

Selecting your rentals is easy. Search by title, category or isbn. Can't find what you're looking for? Send us a request and we'll order it for you.

Delivery

While checking out, schedule your preferred delivery date and time. Choose the best 3-hour block and we'll text you when we're close.

Return

When your return date approaches we'll send you a reminder. If your payment method required a deposit, you'll be refunded at that time.

Pick Up

We'll send you a form to schedule the most convenient pick up time. Simply pick a 3-hour time block and we'll text you when we're nearby.

{# #####Sell###### #}

Search

Enter the isbn of your textbook into the search bar. The isbn is your 13-digit number typically found in the cover or near the barcode.

Quote

It may take a moment but our website will calculate your book's value. If you accept, you'll be directed to a form to provide additional information.

Pick Up

You'll be asked to provide your preferred pick up date and time. Choose the best 3-hour block and we'll text you when we're close.

Payment

Payment will be processed at the time of pick-up. We currently payout via: Paypal, Venmo and Square Cash.
